Beeautiful Bloom

Buy flowers & Be Happy

    Adress: Flowerstreet 21, Gardencity 43392, Flowerland
    Open: 11-15 every day
    About us:
    Beeautiful Bloom, is a charming flower shop offering fresh, seasonal bouquets for every occasion. Each arrangement is handmade with care to brighten your day or someone else’s. You can buy flowers, learn about gardening and even create your own garden! Welcome!
    Mary:
    Mary is a warm and friendly florist at Beeautiful Bloom. With a love for flowers and an eye for design, she creates beautiful arrangements that bring smiles to customers every day.
    Patrick:
    Patrick is a skilled and cheerful florist at Beeautiful Bloom. Known for his creative flair and attention to detail, he helps customers find the perfect bouquet for any occasion.
    How to find us:
    Beeautiful Bloom is located on the corner of Main Street and Oak Avenue, right next to the bakery. From the town square, walk two blocks north on Main Street—it’s the shop with the green awning and flower displays out front.
